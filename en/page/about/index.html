Brief Introduction of the Leader of the Task Force

Xu Ruihua, M.D. ( M.D. ), Ph. D.).Director of the cancer prevention and treatment center of Sun Yat-sen University;baijie, the new century talents of the Ministry of Education, Member of the Department of Biology and Medicine of the Science and Technology Commission of the Ministry of Education, vice president of the Chinese Society of Clinical Oncology ( CSCO ), and vice director of the China Medical Biotechnology Association chairman of the Guangdong Anti-Cancer Association, chairman of the targeted therapy Committee of the China Anti-Cancer Association, and the chemotherapy section of the large intestine cancer committee of the China Anti-Cancer Associationteam leader,Journal Of Chief editor of cancer.National food and Drug Administration (CFDA) drug review consultant expert.


Professor Xu Ruihua has long been engaged in the field of individual treatment of gastrointestinal cancer and anti-cancer drug research, metastasis and prognosis in the digestive tract, chemotherapy drug tolerance and its mechanisms and optimization of clinical treatment with the international leading innovative results, the treatment of patients with 5-year survival rate up to the international leading level.In the field of individual treatment of gastrointestinal cancer, a series of exploration has been carried out, and several international and domestic multicenter clinical studies of anti-tumor drugs have been carried out, the overall survival time of patients with advanced gastric cancer and bowel cancer was significantly prolonged. The results of several studies have been cited by the NCCN guidelines of the United States, and have been widely used in the world to guide the medication program for patients with advanced gastrointestinal cancer.In 2015, entrusted by the state health and Family Planning Commission, as head of the internal medicine group, he led the formulation of the “Chinese colorectal cancer diagnosis and treatment standards” and the compilation of the “colorectal cancerthe consensus of clinical practice of liver metastasis MDT and the lead formulation of the ” consensus of Chinese experts on the maintenance treatment of metastatic colorectal cancer “ were popularized and applied in China. In addition, professor xu ruihua, a member of the expert group of key projects of precision medicine, which is a key research and development program of 135 countries, aims to further realize the individualization of cancer patients treatment, through a large sample of evidence-based medical data means, efforts to promote the precise treatment of cancer process.



Professor Xu Ruihua, as the first person to complete the project, won the second prize of national scientific and technological progress in 2016 and three first prizes at the provincial and ministerial levels (one first prize of the Chinese Medical Association for scientific and technological progress;guangdong province science and technology progress first prize 1 item and Ministry of education institutions of higher learning scientific research outstanding achievement first prize 1 item).In 2007, he was selected as the outstanding talents of the new century by the Ministry of education, and in 2016, he was selected as the outstanding talents of the special talents support program of Guangdong Province (one hundred outstanding talents training project in southern Guangdong).


In the past five years, a total of 79 SCI papers have been published by the (CO) correspondents, and many of them have been published in important international academic journals.Such as nat Mater, Lancet Oncol, J. Clin Oncol, Cell Research, Nat Commun, Hepatology, Ann. Oncol, PNAs, cancer Res and other authoritative journals.


Brief introduction of the research group

Professor Xu Ruihua’s research group focuses on the basic research and transformation of digestive system tumors (colorectal cancer, gastric cancer, and liver cancer).The aim of this study was to investigate the biological characteristics of malignant tumors, such as recurrence and metastasis, resistance to chemotherapy and metabolic disorders, and to elucidate the pathogenesis of malignant tumors.And develop novel targeted and immunotherapeutic strategies.The State Key Laboratory of oncology in South China has complete experimental conditions and a good research platform.
