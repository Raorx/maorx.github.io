The second term of “Famous National Doctor”.

 Posted on August 11, 2018  |  3 minutes (1332 words)  •  Other languages: zh

Professor Xu Ruihua was awarded the second term of “Famous National Doctor” The Second National Famous Doctors Summit, sponsored by People’s Daily and Health Times, was held in Beijing People’s Daily. Professor Xu Ruihua, Director and Dean of CancerControl Center of Sun Yat-sen University, was awarded the honorary title of “National Doctor, Outstanding Style”. Prof. Lu Taixiang, Senior Expert of Nasopharyngeal Carcinoma, and Prof. Zhang Fujun, Associate Director of Minimally Invasive Surgery, were honored with the title of “Distinguished Doctor, Distinguished Master of Chinese Medicine”. It is reported that there are 8 experts in Sun Yat-sen University who have been selected to the top of the Affiliated Hospital System of CUHK with 3 middle and large seats.

Today’s People’s Daily News Center New Media Center is packed with coffee and stars. Lu Yansong, deputy editor-in-chief of People’s Daily, Academician Wang Longde, former Vice Minister of Health and President of China Preventive Medicine Association, and Inspector Li Linkang, Medical Affairs Administration Bureau of National Health Commission attended the meeting and made a speech.

The theme of this year’s State Famous Medical Festival is “Promoting Doctors as Role Models, Leading Medical Opinions and Promoting People’s Health and Well-being” United Chinese Medical Association, Chinese Society of Preventive Medicine, Chinese Medical Association and so on recommended the publication of the list of national famous medical doctors, show the image of national famous medical group.

The list of famous doctors in China includes four honorary titles: Great Physician of China (aged 65 and above), Great Doctor of China (aged 55 and above), Outstanding Doctor of China (aged 45 and above), and Young Doctor Xinrui (aged 45 and under) In recognition of the outstanding clinical work, scientific research contributions, patients have a good reputation for the group of doctors.

The list was selected by Wu Mengchao, Academician of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, former vice president of the Chinese Medical Association, and director of the Oriental Hepatobiliary Surgery Hospital of the Navy Medical University honorary Chairman. Wang Longde, former Vice Minister of public health and President of the Chinese Preventive Medicine Association; Zhang Yanling, President of the Chinese Medical Association; and Ge Junjun, chairman of the cardiovascular disease branch of the Chinese Medical Association.Academician Bo is the co-chairman of the conference, and at the same time 60 specialized branches of the Chinese Medical Association, the Chinese Preventive Medicine Association, and the Chinese Medical Association are the joint sponsors of the conference.The Academic Committee of the Conference is composed of the chairman of the Chinese Medical Association and the relevant chapters of the Chinese Medical Doctor Association, etc, to ensure the authority of the list, objective and fair. A total of 362 experts were evaluated throughout the country .

Introduction of the winners

Professor Xu Ruihua : leading the development of disciplines, creating industry standards, so that the cure of cancer from the “ impossible ” step by step toward possible.

Director, Dean, Professor, Chief Physician and Doctoral Supervisor of Cancer Center of Sun Yat-sen University, chief expert of colorectal cancer. He is currently a member of the biology and medicine division of the science and Technology Commission of the Ministry of education, vice chairman of the China Anti-Cancer Association, and vice chairman of the China Society of Clinical Oncology (CSCO).Won the “southern Guangdong hundred Jie”, the national health family planning outstanding contribution of young experts, the National hundreds of millions of engineering talent, Guangdong Province, advanced individual labor and other honorary titles. We won the second prize of the national scientific and technological progress, and the first prize of the provincial and ministerial level scientific and technological progress.

Professor Xu Ruihua led the team into the clinical first-line treatment of patients with a “ medical parents heart “, and set up a large team as academic leaders, in view of the difficulties in diagnosis and treatment, scientific research is carried out to tackle difficult problems, and the 5-year survival rate of the patients treated is up to the international leading level, and the patients are highly recognized and widely praised. For the first time in the world, the methylation molecular markers that can be used for early diagnosis and prognosis prediction of liver cancer have been defined, and a new method for diagnosis and screening of liver cancer has been established, the diagnostic accuracy was increased from 81.6 % to 96.9 % of the existing liver cancer serum marker AFP, and the missed diagnosis rate was reduced by more than half.

For the first time in the world, a new first-line and third-line treatment plan for advanced colorectal cancer was established, and a new system for the whole course of treatment was established, and the maintenance treatment of digestive system tumor ( bowel cancer ) was put forward new targeted therapy offers new therapeutic possibilities for patients with advanced bowel cancer, extending the median survival from 13 months to 27 months.It is the first time to find several high-frequency mutation genes which are closely related to esophageal cancer in the world.It is the first internationally to improve the targeted therapy strategy for advanced gastric cancer, and significantly prolong the survival time of patients.

People’s Daily reports on the research results of Xu Ruihua’s team

Professor Xu Ruihua’s four research achievements on digestive tract cancer are cited by the American NCCN Guidelines, and are widely applied in the world. In 2015, commissioned by the National Health and Family Planning Commission, as the head of the internal medicine group led the development of the “ Chinese colorectal cancer diagnosis and treatment norms, ” As the editor-in-chief of “ colorectal cancer. ” the consensus of clinical practice of MDT of liver metastasis and the six diagnosis and treatment norms that lead to the formulation of “ Expert Consensus on the Application of Capecitabine Maintenance Therapy for Advanced Colorectal Cancer ” were popularized in China application, guiding the medication plan of patients with advanced digestive tract cancer, benefiting millions of patients with digestive system tumor, and resulting in a good social benefit.

Xu Ruihua’s team research results won the national science and technology progress second prize

At the same time, Professor Xu Ruihua, director of the Guangdong Provincial Cancer Center, undertakes the province’s cancer prevention and control technical guidance function, and has built a grassroots hospital in Guangzhou, a new network of mass prevention and treatment for disease control agencies and large specialized hospitals at all levels; free screening of five common cancers ( lung cancer, liver cancer, upper gastrointestinal cancer, colorectal cancer, and breast cancer ) among urban residents reached 50,000 per year .

As a member of the Professional Committee of Targeting Therapy of China Cancer Society, actively promoting the concept of diagnosis and treatment of tumor targeted therapy and the diagnosis and treatment of targeted therapy, lead the development of tumor targeted therapy and precision medicine in China. As the director of the Guangdong Provincial Anticancer Association, actively organize and promote the anti-cancer associations and various professional committees throughout the province to actively carry out cancer prevention and health education every year, there are hundreds of large free clinics and lectures.

In September 2017, under the leadership of Xu Ruihua, the center led the formation of the first largest tumor specialist alliance in China at that time, and now covers 64 provinces home hospital, through offline technical assistance, online remote consultation and other means, effectively promoted the level of tumor prevention and control of grassroots hospitals.

In recent years, Professor Xu Ruihua has been involved in the preparation of the national cancer prevention and control strategy, including the “ Research Program for Cancer Prevention and Control ” in the health protection project, the Development Strategy of China’s Oncology Precision Medicine and the Medical and Health Science and Technology Strategy in the Research of the Development Strategy of the Centennial Science and Technology Power, actively contribute to the strategy of healthy China, and contribute to it.
